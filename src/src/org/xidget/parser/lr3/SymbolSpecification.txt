Symbol Specification

The specification for symbols on the right-hand side of a rule will support the following:

  - Single symbols either by character, or decimal/hexadecimal value.
  - Strings of symbols by character.
  - Ranges of symbols either by character, or decimal/hexadecimal value.
  - Characters to be ignored/consumed at specific position in rule (warning message indicates all affected rules that do not contain specification).
  - Characters to be ignored/consumed within the handle of a non-terminal.
